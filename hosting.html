    FAQs

    Are there any hidden fees that I should know about?

    Nope! We believe in "what you see is what you get", and that's how we run every aspect of King Code. You will get exactly what is mentioned at the price it is mentioned for.

    Can I pay for hosting per month?

    Since all of our subscriptions are billed yearly, your website hosting plan is billed yearly too. One month before your website hosting plan expires, you'll receive an email from us. You will then be reminded two weeks later, then 3 days before expiration. So you will be reminded from us plenty of times.

    Can I transfer my current hosting to King Code hosting?

    Yes, of course, and the process is pretty simple depending on your current provider. Please email us here informing us on who your current provider is and we'll give you the instructions on how to easily transfer you domain name and hosting.

    How much does blogging cost?

    There is no additional hosting fee for blogs with our hosting plans. Our hosting fees are the same for all website types. The only difference is whether you'd like email hosting too.

    How much does an ecommerce store cost?

    There is no additional hosting fee for ecommerce websites with our hosting plans. Since we use Shopify Lite, you will be charged directly by Shopify for using their payment gateway usually around $9 a month, but as for our hosting and CMS / web builder, your plan will not increase in price. Our hosting fees are the same for all website types. The only difference is whether you'd like email hosting too.
